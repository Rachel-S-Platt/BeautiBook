The #1 app to organize, share, and discover makeup looks

Organize:

Beautibook gives you an organized interface to add makeup looks to your look books and input/store information such as: (1) exactly what products and shades you used and where you used them, (2) the specific steps you took to achieve this look, (3) and attach photos to the look.

Share:

You can easily share any look you have stored in your look books through a direct link or through social media. You can also follow your favorite beauty gurus to view their makeup looks (including exactly what products they used and what steps they took)!

Discover:

BeautiBook allows you to discover new looks and artists. You can search for makeup inspiration by searching through other people's looks filtering by product, color, eyeshadow shape, and more.
